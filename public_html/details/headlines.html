Headlines by Vinay Goel

Headlines is a set of timelapse videos generated from 20 years of the Wayback Machine.

20 Years

Time lapse of the daily homepages of three major US newspapers, usatoday.com, nytimes.com and washingtonpost.com, from Nov 12, 1996 to Sep 30, 2016. Contains a total of 4482 snapshots.

Aug/Sep 2016

Time lapse of the daily homepages of the top three US newspapers by daily circulation, usatoday.com, nytimes.com and wsj.com

Cable News Late 2016
